Credit Checks

Credit checks can be of great value to your business prior to offering credit accounts to any of your customers.

This is something that can easily be overlooked when attempting to get a new customer on board. There are several, simple cost effective ways that enable you to carry these checks out yourself to provide you with peace of mind.

We offer a more comprehensive Credit Check for bigger Customers as you will have to put your trust in them and need to make sure that they will not suddenly liquidate or close down anytime soon. We can also then keep a regular check and alert you of any changes in their circumstances when and if they arise.
