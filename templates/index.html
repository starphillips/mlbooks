About Us

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

Every business, no matter how big or small, needs to keep complete record of all income and expenses going through the business. It is a further requirement that copies of all these transactions are kept for at least 6 years. Sounds simple enough, but it can often get out of control.


This is where ML Bookkeepers can help. Making sure your Customers are invoiced sooner rather than later means getting paid faster. This will in turn improve your cash flow so that you can afford the day to day expenses of running your business, thus providing you with working capital.


The other benefit of keeping up to date with your income and expense transaction is that you will have a good idea of your businesses cash flow. This will also help to see where the business is making or losing money so that you can respond quickly to avoid a further waste of finances and take advantage of the more profitable opportunities. Keeping complete records of all your income and expenses on a timely basis will keep your accounts up to date for Self Assessment, VAT and Annual Returns to be filed quickly and accurately.
